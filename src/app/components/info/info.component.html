November 29, 2025

08:30 am - 05:00 pm

Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar

DevFest Jalandhar is one of the biggest tech conferences in North India. It is a completely volunteer-led and community-driven conference hosted by GDG Jalandhar. The event will have multiple tracks and sessions on different technologies and will be conducted by industry experts. The motive of this event is to bring the Developer Community of Punjab and Chandigarh under one roof, providing them chance to network with each other and industry experts.
